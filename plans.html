Individual Health Insurance

Individual health insurance can be adjutsted according to the needs of the person.It is a health plan which can be purchased by a person him/herself to maintain their health and take care of medical care whenever required.It offers medical coverage to the person on an individual health sum insured basis.

Family Health Insurance

A family health insurance plan is a kind of medical insurance that covers all your family members in a single health insurance policy.This includes the spouse , dependant children and dependant parents. A family health insurance plan is a cost-effective and offers medical coverage to the entire family at a single premium.



Critical Illness Insurance

Critical illness plan provides coverage against specific life-threatening diseases.Critical illness insurance provides coverage for medical emergencies like heart attacks, strokes, or cancer.This plan supports the person financially if he/she is diagnosed with one of the conditions described included in the policy.

Senior Citizen Health Insurance

Senior citizen health insurance plan is designed to offer financial aid for medical treatments to individuals over 60 years of age at a highly affordable price.This plan offers financial aid coverage for medical charges,including hospital and surgery costs and medical expenses.



Personal Accident Insurance

Accident insurance is by far the most crucial insurance policy.It offers monetary support to the insured person in case of accident.This cover is provided for compensation against death and bodily injuries caused by violence, accident, or by other external and visible means.
